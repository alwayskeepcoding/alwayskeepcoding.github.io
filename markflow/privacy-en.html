Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MarkFlow ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the MarkFlow: AI Format Engine mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Information We Collect

MarkFlow does not collect or transmit any personal information from users. The App operates completely offline and processes all data locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal identification information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal data stored on your device
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Use any analytics services to track your usage

Local Data Storage

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Markdown content you input for conversion
  • Conversion history for your convenience
  • User preferences and settings

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the App's history within the App or by uninstalling the App.

App Permissions

MarkFlow may request the following permissions:

  • Storage access: To save converted documents to your device
  • Photo library access: To save generated images to your photo library (optional)

These permissions are only used for the stated purposes and no data is collected, stored, or transmitted.

Children's Privacy

Our App is rated 4+ (suitable for all ages) on the App Store. While the App is designed to be appropriate for users of all ages, we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. As the App operates completely offline and processes all data locally on your device, no personal information is collected or shared.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
